Methodology, costs & sources ▶

All CO₂, financial, and deployment figures are calibrated for a 5 MW mid-size data center baseline and scale proportionally with the facility size selector. CapEx, savings, and acquisition time estimates are drawn from peer-reviewed studies, government reports, and vendor case studies — suitable for order-of-magnitude business case planning, not formal capital budgeting. IRA tax credits, utility rebates, and solar PPAs can materially improve ROI. EPA GHG Equivalencies Calculator factors underpin all tree, car, and flight equivalencies. PUE baseline of 1.58 and WUE baseline of 1.80 reflect the 2023 global industry averages per Uptime Institute Annual Data Center Survey. Slot infrastructure cost of $250k/slot at 5 MW baseline reflects real-world rack provisioning costs (PDU, cooling hookups, structured cabling, floor space) per industry operator estimates.

All figures are illustrative estimates for educational and planning purposes only. Break-even calculations assume constant energy prices and exclude maintenance costs, equipment degradation, and policy incentives. Time-to-deploy estimates reflect typical project timelines; actual timelines depend on vendor availability, permitting, utility coordination, and organizational readiness. Consult a certified energy auditor and licensed electrical engineer for facility-specific projections.
